Featured Product

Allora FPGA

The only tool you need for coding, simulating, synthesizing, and programming open-source FPGA boards — a complete bring-up workflow in one polished desktop application.

Open-Source Toolchains

Synthesize with Yosys and place & route with nextpnr. A fully open flow with no proprietary lock-in.

Visual Pin Mapping

Browse, search, and assign pins on an interactive board diagram with live constraint generation.

Synthesis & Bitstream

Compile, synthesize, and generate bitstreams with real-time logs and a clean, distraction-free editor.

Hardware Programming

Flash your design directly to the board with one click and supported programmers built right in.

Health Monitoring

Live Serial Monitoring and board health insights keep your iteration loop tight and observable.

Multi-Board Support

iCEBreaker, ULX3S, OrangeCrab, Fomu, Colorlight and more — dozens of boards out of the box.
