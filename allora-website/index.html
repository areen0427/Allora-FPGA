Allora FPGA

A complete bench for open FPGA development. Write RTL, simulate, synthesize, route, and program your board without ever leaving the application or reaching for a proprietary toolchain.

01

Open Toolchain, End to End

Yosys for synthesis, nextpnr for place and route. A fully open flow with nothing proprietary in the path and nothing to lock you in.

02

Visual Pin Mapping

Assign signals on an interactive board diagram. Search, browse, and generate constraints as you go, in both guided and advanced views.

03

Synthesis to Bitstream

Compile, synthesize, place, route, and pack a bitstream with live logs at every stage — all from one clean, focused editor.

04

One-Click Programming

Flash directly to your board with the right programmer detected for you. Source to running silicon in a single action.

05

Live Diagnostics

Serial monitoring and board-health readouts keep every iteration observable, so you can see what the hardware is actually doing.

06

Built for Real Boards

iCEBreaker, ULX3S, OrangeCrab, Fomu, Colorlight and more — dozens of supported boards, configured and ready out of the box.
